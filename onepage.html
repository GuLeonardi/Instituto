Who we are

Instituto Península is a social organization that works in Education and Sports to improve teacher training because we understand that teachers are the basis for quality public education. We work on the integral development of teachers in the physical, intellectual, emotional, and social dimensions so that they are better prepared to" strengthen students' learning process. We think and act systematically, seeking partnerships to develop Brazilian education.

At Instituto Península, we believe it is essential to unite the best of existing theories about teachers with the profession in everyday life to execute our actions. Our organization supports four initiatives that are linked to the purpose of transforming lives through education, sports, and the integral development of the individual, and develops projects that help the educational training of teachers and take a systemic approach to the career.
